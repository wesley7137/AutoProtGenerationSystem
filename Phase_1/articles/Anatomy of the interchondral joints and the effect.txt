Title: Anatomy of the interchondral joints and the effects on mobility of ribs.
Abstract: Variations in the anatomy of the anterior rib cage and costal margin have been observed. We sought to evaluate the location of interchondral joints and evaluate their effect on mobility of the rib cage.
