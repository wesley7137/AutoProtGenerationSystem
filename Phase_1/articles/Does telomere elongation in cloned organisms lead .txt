Title: Does telomere elongation in cloned organisms lead to a longer lifespan
  if cancer is considered ?
Abstract:   ... By additionally considering a two-mutation model for carcinogenesis and
indefinite proliferation by the activation of telomerase, we demonstrate that
the risk of dying doe to cancer can outweigh the positive effect of longer
telomeres on the longevity.
